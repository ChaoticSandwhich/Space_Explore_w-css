Space Suit Expenses

What makes them so expensive?

Think about them in terms of spacecraft, not as work clothes. A spacesuit has to protect an astronaut from the vacuum of space, from radiation coming from the sun and other bodies, and it has to protect against fast-traveling particles that are traveling up to 18,000 miles an hour that could penetrate the suit.

How much do they cost?

NASA's new spacesuits cost $3.5 billion. Axiom won a $228 million contract last year to create a suit for the Artemis III mission in 2025. Collins Aerospace was awarded $97.2 million for an ISS spacesuit.
